Birds of Bangladesh



Bangladesh is a beautiful country. Birds have increased its beauty more. We sleep at night and rise very early in the morning hearing the sweet songs of different birds in Bangladesh. There are different types of birds in our beautiful country. They are different in color, size, and habit. They are divided into many classes. The crow is a teasing bird. It is a very common bird in Bangladesh. It is very clever. It looks ugly. Yet it is useful. It feeds on dead animals and dirty things. The kite is also known for the same habit and nature.The cuckoo, the shyama, the doel, the koel, the mayna, the nightingale, etc are known as songbirds.
